Services

Detailed breakdown of technical and creative services offered.

3D Asset Creation

I provide high-quality, game-ready 3D assets tailored to your project's specific art style and technical requirements. From hard-surface props to organic environments, every asset is optimized for real-time rendering.

  • Modeling: High-poly to Low-poly workflow with clean topology.
  • Texturing: PBR workflow (Substance Painter) for realistic or stylized materials.
  • Optimization: LOD generation, collision meshes, and UV packing.

Level Design & Lighting

Creating immersive worlds that guide the player and tell a story. I focus on flow, composition, and atmosphere to ensure the environment supports the gameplay loop.

  • Greyboxing: Rapid prototyping of level layouts for gameplay testing.
  • Set Dressing: Environmental storytelling using modular kits and props.
  • Lighting: Baked and real-time lighting setups for mood and visibility.

Technical Art

Bridging the gap between art and code. I help optimize pipelines and create visual effects that run smoothly on your target hardware.

  • Shaders: Custom shaders using HLSL or Shader Graph (Unity/Unreal).
  • Profiling: Identifying and fixing performance bottlenecks (Draw calls, Overdraw).
  • Tools: Creating editor scripts to speed up the art workflow.

Courses

Comprehensive courses designed to take you from beginner to pro. Learn industry-standard workflows with practical, project-based lessons.

  • Godot Engine Course: Master the open-source engine. From GDScript basics to advanced systems.
  • 3D Modeling Course: Learn the full pipeline: Modeling, UVs, Texturing, and Engine Implementation.
  • Mentorship: 1-on-1 guidance and code reviews to accelerate your learning.

Art Commissions

Unique digital artwork for your creative projects or personal collection.

  • Music Cover Art: Eye-catching visuals for your singles, EPs, and albums. Tailored to your genre.
  • Art Prints: High-quality prints of my personal work available for purchase.
  • Custom Illustration: Character art and stylized environments.
